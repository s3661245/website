My Project Idea

Speech to Speech translation application called Speak2Me

What is Speak2Me?

My project idea is called Speak2Me which is a mobile application that can translate foreign audio into any other language as an audio output using an AI system. For example, if you’re working in retail and a customer that comes in isn’t proficient in English, the manager will be able to use the application to understand the customer an be able to assist them effectively as they now will be able to understand exactly what the customer wants. This product should be available on Google Play and the Apple App Store to ensure that most mobile platforms should be able to access it.

Why this project?

Speak2Me will be able to connect people from different and diverse cultures with little effort. Businesses will be able to understand their international customers more and suit their needs to what they supply, teachers can go overseas and be able to educate kids in third world countries to improve their overall quality of life and even average living citizen will be able to access the application and be able to understand what another person from a different country is saying. There are over 7000 languages in the world in which 25 make up 50% of this world speaking population. I think it’ll be interesting to do as it’ll connect people from diverse cultures and society’s together; making this world more unified one step at a time.

What makes Speak2Me unique (Part 1)?

This application will be accessible via Google Play and the Apple App Store and it’ll be free to use. In terms of android devices, it will be able to run on android Lollipop and above and with IOS devices, it will be compatible with IOS 7.1 and above. Once an individual will tap on the application a screen will come up showing what language is going to be spoken and what language the user would want it to be translated to. In doing so, the AI system will be connected to the internet to be able to search up specific words and their translation to be able to interpret and translate all words spoken including slang that most translators won’t be able to detect and interpret. As the AI system learns word patterns it’ll gradually become more efficient in detecting phrases and their meaning and string which will increase the overall user’s experience with the program.

What makes Speak2Me unique (Part 2)?

As the AI system develops, due to the fact that it is stored on the internet it’ll be able to simultaneously help multiple users at the same time and pick up language from all users efficiently and effectively. The application will also have a “detect language” function in which the app will detect the language spoken and have the ability to output in to whatever language the user has set. There is also a premium offline option in which the user will be able to download language packages for use offline and away from the internet which will not be involving the AI system but instead it’ll be using a stored internal dictionary.

What makes Speak2Me unique(Part 3)?

The internal dictionary’s will be sourced from the google translate dictionary’s which will also be stored on the cloud for direct download from the online servers. The recording can be played through the phone or any external devices such as headphones or wireless speakers. The recording can be inputted through either an internal microphone already on the mobile device or an external microphone via a headset.
